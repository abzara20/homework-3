Perspective of "San-X" business

San-X Co., Ltd. create unique and fun characters that are adored by fans around the world.
Based in Tokyo, San-X is one of the largest character manufacturing comapny in Japan, specializing in stationary, plush, and accessory goods.
The blue in San-X represents youth, novelty, challenge, and the future. The four-leaf clover symbolizes good luck.
